About


The UW Financial Analysis & Risk Management Student Association was formed in 2011 with a goal to provide interested students with the knowledge, tools, and networks needed to start a successful career in finance and/or risk management. The FARM program combines courses in mathematics, statistics, and computer science with corporate finance, asset pricing, micro- and macroeconomics and other business-related courses to provide students a well-rounded background in the quantitative and qualitative aspects of the financial industry. Our students have attained placements in Market Risk, Credit Risk, Investment Banking, Sales and Trading, Venture Capital, Asset Management and many more.

FARMSAâ€™s purpose is to encourage academic pursuits and interests of the Math/FARM program both internally within the faculty of Mathematics at the University of Waterloo and externally, by connecting students with industry professionals. FARMSA also provides services that benefit Math/FARM students and other interested individuals academically and professionally. The student organization works closely with the faculty of Mathematics as well as other associations within the University of Waterloo to improve the cohesiveness between individuals of the Math/FARM program and provide a framework to build, improve and promote the reputation and quality of the Math/FARM program.
